tube2blog

Create a companion blog from any YouTube video

How it works

Submit a link to any YouTube video.
We use Large Language Models like OpenAI's Whisper to extract a transcript of your video.
Then we post process the output to make it easy for you to use.
Now you've created twice the content without doing double the work.

Why blog

Reading is faster than skipping through a video.
Companion blogs can improve the ranking of your videos in search engines and on YouTube.
Drive traffic to your website from YouTube by using a blog.

Why us
