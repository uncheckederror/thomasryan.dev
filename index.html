Ballard High School

In 2011 I graduated from Ballard High School where I was a student in the Biotechnology Academy and enjoyed classes in Video Production and AP US History.

Seattle Central

In 2010 I enrolled in the Running Start program which allow me to take classes at Seattle Central Community College while I was still in High School.

During this period I began working as a writer and photographer for both the Student newspaper and SemiAccurate.com. At the Seattle Central I covered campus events and did faculty interviews. For SemiAccurate I wrote product reviews for computer hardware and compiled news roundups.

University of Washington - Seattle Campus

In 2013 I was admitted to the University of Washington's Seattle campus where I found the right environment to explore my love for Geography and Mapping.

Professionally this was an exciting time for me as I began writing freelance story for PCWorld.com which receives an order of magnitude more page views than any platform I'd published on previously.

The Summer Food Service Program

Starting in 2012 and ending in 2015 I spent my summers working for the City of Seattle's Human Services Department.

In this role I spend my first two summers speeding around the City in an old van delivering boxes of pre-made meals to parks and community centers for distribution to hungry children. The last two summers I worked as a distribution site manager where I handled the training of the distribution staff and the filing of their accounting paper work.
